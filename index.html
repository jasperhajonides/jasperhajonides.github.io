About

Timeline, from present to 2012.

  • 2017-2020

    PhD at Oxford University

    Supervised by Prof. Kia Nobre and Prof. Mark Stokes my doctoral research investigates the role of top-down modulations on visual representations in the brain.

  • Nov 2016 - Aug 2017

    Visiting research scientist at Princeton University

    Working with Prof. Sabine Kastner I completed an MRI study investigating perception and attention in visual cortex and pulvinar.

  • Jan 2016 - Nov 2016

    Donders Institute

    Visiting research scientist

    At the Donders Institute I worked with Prof. Floris de Lange to decode neural correlates of visual expectations and visual short-term memory using functional MRI with high temporal frequency.

  • Sep 2014 - Aug 2015

    Oxford University

    Undergraduate internship

    In collaboration with Dr. Robert Mok and Prof. Kia Nobre, I worked on a behavioural and eye-tracking study investigating emotional working memory in younger and older adults.

  • Sep 2012 - Aug 2015

    BSc at University of Amsterdam

    After finishing high school I pursued a degree in Psychobiology at the University of Amsterdam. This research-oriented programme taught courses on neurobiology, neuropharmacalogy, statistics, cognitive psychology, social cognition, and more. I completed an excellence programme and finished all taught courses in two years and moved to Oxford for an internship during my third year
